# Scope 3 Tracker: Research Background Summary
## For PhD Application & Research Proposals

---

## Project Overview (150 words)

**Scope 3 Tracker** is an AI-powered carbon emissions monitoring platform that achieves **90%+ coverage** of indirect supply chain emissions through integrated data fusion. The platform combines **machine learning** (Isolation Forest anomaly detection, spend-based estimation), **IoT real-time monitoring**, and **blockchain verification** to solve the critical problem of incomplete and unreliable emission data. Built with Django/Python, it demonstrates expertise in **multi-source data integration**, **ML model deployment**, and **serverless cloud architecture** (AWS Lambda). The platform's ML-driven hotspot detection and scenario modeling capabilities directly translate to CO2 storage monitoring, leakage detection, and geomechanical risk assessment—making it highly relevant for PhD research in CO2 monitoring, geomechanics, and seismic analysis.
